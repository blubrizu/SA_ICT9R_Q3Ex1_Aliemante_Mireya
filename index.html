TARTAGLIA PHASES

Character Phases Description
Tartaglia Phase One: Hydro In this phase, he uses his hydroblades and his bow (infused with the hydro element).
Tartaglia Phase Two: Electro In this phase, his powers change from hydro to electro, yet his attacks stay the same (electroblades and bow).
Tartaglia Phase Three: Boss Fight (Electro & Hydro) This is the very last phase of Tartaglia, in this phase he can use both hydro and electro powers. He can summon a big whale as an attack and can teleport towards you.
